Center Support Rule

At 28" or greater DEPTH, shelves require a 3rd center column of pin holes to prevent sagging under load.

  • This shelf: 30" deep → Center support required
  • Pin columns on cabinet sides: Front (2"), Center (15"), Back (2" from back = 28")
  • Notches: 6 total (4 corners + 2 center)
  • Pins: 6 total (one per notch)
