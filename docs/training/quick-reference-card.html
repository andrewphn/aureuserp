üìö Common Cabinet Terms

Recessed area at bottom of base cabinet (4.5" high)

Cabinet width in feet, used for pricing
Inset vs Overlay
Door mounting: flush inside frame vs covering frame
Soft Close
Hardware that gently closes doors/drawers
Edge Banding
Thin strip covering plywood edges
